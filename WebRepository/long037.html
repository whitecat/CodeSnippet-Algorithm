Product Description

Long pepper is a close relative of black pepper and has a similar taste, with a strong biting aftertaste. This pepper was an important spice in Europe before the European discovery of the New World. Long pepper has sweet and fragrant aroma, contradicting its hot, lingering, and numbing taste. *Check out the other peppers, salts, and chocolates at The Meadow on Amazon!
