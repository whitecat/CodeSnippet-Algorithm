Hi,

heres the way
mybyte[0] =(byte)( foo >> 24 );
mybyte[1] =(byte)( (foo << 8) >> 24 );
mybyte[2] =(byte)( (foo << 16) >> 24 );
mybyte[3] =(byte)( (foo << 24) >> 24 );
