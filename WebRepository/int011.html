Interactive Network Technologies, Inc. is a leading provider of high performance software components for visualization and analysis of complex data. INT products span a variety of development environments, including C++, Java, .NET, and C (X/Motif). They provide exceptional performance and cross-platform functionality, enabling software engineers to reduce development times and ongoing costs while enhancing the performance and functionality of their graphics applications.

For over sixteen years, organizations both large and small have employed INT products and services. In industries as diverse as oil and gas, telecommunications, defense, medical imaging, and finance, INT offers solutions for GIS visualization, scientific and business charting, data monitoring, and other business critical endeavors requiring sophisticated presentation and interaction with complex data.
