Beaver long jump: A free java long jump game for you to play online

In this free long jump game, the object is simply to jump as far as possible. You get three attempts to improve your distance. This athletic sport is contested in the olympic summer games.

Sorry - you need java to play this free java long jump game. Go get a browser with java support, then come back here and play some free games.
Click the mouse once to start the nice beaver running, then again to make him jump. Click once more to choose the beavers angle of takeoff (around 45 degrees is best).
